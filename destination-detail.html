01 — Destination

A World Unto Itself

Scattered across the equator like jewels dropped from a great height, the Maldives is an archipelago of 1,200 coral islands arranged in 26 natural atolls. Barely above sea level, impossibly blue, and extraordinarily biodiverse — this is one of the world's last truly unspoilt ocean landscapes.

Farland's Maldives experiences are entirely bespoke. Whether you're seeking a remote private-island resort, a liveaboard diving safari, or an overwater honeymoon villa with a butler, we design your stay around your exact vision.
